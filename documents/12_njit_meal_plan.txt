# Meal Plan Requirement and Tech Bucks
First year and Sophomores (by credit hour) living on campus are required to have a meal plan, selecting from plans A-E. Tech Bucks work like a debit card, with purchases deducted from your balance, and may be used at all on-campus Gourmet Dining eateries. Tech Bucks carry over from the fall to spring semester but not from spring to fall; you must use all Tech Bucks by the last day of exams or you forfeit them. Rates are per person per semester (academic year 2025-2026) and are updated for the upcoming academic year by the end of July.

# First Year and Sophomore Resident Dining Plans
All first-year and sophomore plans include continuous unlimited dining and 10 guest entries. A Plan ($0 Tech Bucks): $2,440 per semester. B Plan ($100 Tech Bucks): $2,540 per semester. C Plan ($200 Tech Bucks): $2,640 per semester. D Plan ($400 Tech Bucks): $2,840 per semester. E Plan ($600 Tech Bucks): $3,040 per semester.

# Upper-Class Dining Plans
Available to juniors, seniors, graduate students, and commuters. F Plan (80 anytime meals per semester, 5 guest entries, $400 Tech Bucks): $1,615 per semester. G Plan ($1,414 Tech Bucks): $1,414 per semester. H Plan (80 anytime meals per semester, 5 guest entries, $0 Tech Bucks): $1,215 per semester. J Plan (commuter meal plan, 5 meals per week, breakfast and/or lunch only): $950 per semester.
