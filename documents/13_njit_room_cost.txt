# Twelve-Month Housing Contracts
Twelve-month housing contracts, covering winter and summer breaks, cost an extra $1,200 per semester (totaling $2,400) and end July 31 if you are not renewing your housing contract. Contract changes are due by December 31 each year. Rates are updated for the upcoming academic year by the end of July or beginning of August.
