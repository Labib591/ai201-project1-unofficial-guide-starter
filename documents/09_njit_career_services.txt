# Services Offered
The Career Development Services (CDS) provides comprehensive support including resume and cover letter assistance, career advising where students can find their career advisor for personalized guidance, interview preparation resources and workshops, structured career planning services, free professional headshots through PopCam appointment scheduling, and a Career Closet clothing resource for professional development.

# Internships & Co-op
The office facilitates experiential learning opportunities through cooperative education and internship placements, on-campus interviewing programs connecting students with employers, and career fair participation for employer connections.

# Handshake Platform
Students access job and internship opportunities through the Handshake portal, which also hosts appointment scheduling for services, event registration, and employer connections.

# Career Fairs
CDS organizes career fairs featuring employers actively recruiting NJIT graduates.

# Employment Outcomes
Recent graduates secured positions at major companies. One graduate noted: After graduation, I am headed to Merck & Co. for a 2 1/2 year rotational program in IT. The office publishes detailed outcome reports and starting salary data organized by major and degree type.
