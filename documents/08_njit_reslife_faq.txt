# Housing Assignment & Confirmation
Current students not living on campus should submit their housing application when available. Fall applicants participate in online room selection; spring applicants receive assignments after the holiday break via NJIT email. Confirmation emails are sent through NJIT email accounts. Questions should be directed to reslife@njit.edu or 973.596.3039. Housing guarantee: We are unable to guarantee housing for the entire time you are at NJIT.

# Housing Costs & Occupancy
Cost information is available at the Residence Life Rates page. Approximately 2,500 students occupy six co-ed residence halls and the Greek Village.

# First-Year & Upper-Class Housing
First-year students live in Cypress, Redwood, and Martinson Residence Hall (Honors). Upper-class students live in all buildings. All rooms feature furnishings, air conditioning, and WiFi, with common areas including lounges, study spaces, kitchens, and laundry facilities.

# Meal Plans & Food Services
NJIT contracts with an independent vendor offering balanced meals including traditional hot foods, fast foods, soups, salads, deli sandwiches, and desserts. Vegetarian and vegan options are available. Tech Bucks transfer from fall to spring semester; remaining balances at semester end are non-refundable. Food service hours are available at Gourmet Dining Service's website (dineoncampus.com/NJIT).

# Campus Safety & Access
Students use NJIT ID cards for residence hall access. Desk attendants staff entrances 24/7. All guests require valid photo identification and must be signed in by resident hosts, remaining accompanied at all times. Professional police and safety officers patrol campus continuously; rooftop surveillance cameras operate around the clock.

# Resident Assistants
RAs serve as community facilitators, activities organizers, resource persons, peer counselors, administrators, disciplinarians, and friends.

# Roommate Assignment & Conflicts
Roommate requests: Transfer students, new graduate students, and continuing students not participating in room selection receive roommate information in mid-July. First-year students select roommates online in June/July. Room assignment letters are sent mid-July for non-room-selection participants. Students complete roommate agreements upon move-in. Your resident assistant will be available to assist you if you have a concern or conflict.

# Guest Policy
You are allowed to have up to three guests at one time, however there are certain restrictions. Details are available in the Policies and Procedures section.

# On-Campus Employment
Contact the Student Financial Aid Services Office in the Student Mall for job opportunities. Residence Life also hires students; employment positions are listed on their employment page.

# Storage Services
StorageSquad.com (NJIT's exclusive partner) provides free boxes, tape, and labels, with pickup, storage, and fall redelivery to student rooms.

# Housing Accommodations & ESAs
Disability accommodations: NJIT's Residence Life Office, with Office of Accessibility Resources & Services (OARS) support, provides reasonable accommodations. Students must contact OARS at 973-596-5417 or oars@njit.edu with documentation from treating professionals. Emotional Support Animals prescribed by healthcare professionals as part of disability treatment require OARS approval, reviewed on a case-by-case basis by OARS in consultation with the Office of Residence Life.
